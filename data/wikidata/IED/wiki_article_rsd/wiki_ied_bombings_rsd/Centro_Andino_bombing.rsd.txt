On the afternoon of 18 June 2017, a bombing in Bogotá, Colombia, killed three young women and injured 11 other people. One of those killed was French, the other two were Colombian. The bomb exploded behind the toilet bowl in the women's toilets in the upmarket Centro Andino shopping mall in the Zona Rosa district.

==Responsibility==
Police later arrested eight members of a small far-left urban guerilla group called the People’s Revolutionary Movement (Movimiento Revolucionario del Pueblo, MRP). The group has only been known since 2015 and are suspected in 14 other attacks in Colombia.
